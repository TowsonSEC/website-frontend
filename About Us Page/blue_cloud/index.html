Established in 2016, the Software Engineering Club at Towson University is a student run organization open to anyone interested. Whether you have little computer science experience or you have been at for years SEC is right for you! Not only do we create our own projects but, we also run workshops to gain knowledge in areas that are not covered in the classroom to help you now and beyond. Be on the lookout for our upcoming events, we hope to see you there!
